Who Are We ?

We Can is a full-service, community-based Redemption Center (RC), offering an innovative approach to mobile redemption.

As a mobile redemption center company, We Can provides a convenient, accessible, and streamlined option for returning deposit containers, such as redeemable cans and bottles. We serve residents, businesses, institutions, and municipalities, making the process effortless and entirely free of charge.

Our goal is to make recycling easier and more efficient, contributing to a cleaner environment and supporting local communities.
