How it works

WeCan makes recycling simple and rewarding. Register and log in to receive a unique QR code. Schedule a convenient pickup or drop-off time for your cans and bottles. Our team ensures efficient collection, tracks your recycling history, and updates your account balance, allowing you to redeem rewards effortlessly.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is a sustainable future where recycling is second nature to everyone. We aim to empower communities with a user-friendly platform to maximize recycling efforts, reduce waste, and protect our planet for generations to come. Together, we strive to make eco-friendly living easy and accessible.
